Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid as a publisher?

+

We offer multiple payment methods including PayPal, Wire Transfer, and Payoneer. Payments are made monthly, with a minimum threshold of $100. You can track your earnings in real-time through your publisher dashboard and will receive payment around the 15th of each month for the previous month's earnings.

What types of websites can join AdSphere?

+

We accept most content categories including news, entertainment, sports, technology, finance, and more. We do not accept adult content, illegal material, or sites promoting violence/hate speech. All websites go through a manual review process to ensure quality standards.

How does AdSphere compare to other ad networks?

+

AdSphere offers several advantages over other networks:

  • Higher CPM rates due to premium demand partners
  • Advanced fraud protection to ensure clean traffic
  • Wider range of high-performing ad formats
  • Transparent reporting with no hidden fees
  • Dedicated account managers for premium publishers

Can I use AdSphere with other ad networks?

+

Yes, we support header bidding and can work alongside other ad networks through our Header Bidding Wrapper. Our technology will automatically select the highest paying ad from all available demand sources to maximize your revenue. We also provide guidance on optimal ad stack configurations.

What traffic volume do I need to join?

+

We welcome publishers of all sizes. While we have no strict minimum traffic requirements, our platform performs best with websites receiving at least 10,000 monthly pageviews. Smaller sites may be approved but could experience lower fill rates initially. As your traffic grows, our system automatically optimizes to deliver higher performance.
